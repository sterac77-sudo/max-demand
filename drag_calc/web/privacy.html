Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 November 2025

Drag Racing Toolbox ("the App") is provided by Intolerance Racing. This policy describes how the Android app handles data.

Information Collection and Use

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process certain data to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • Device type and operating system

For more information about how Google uses data, see: Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses data when you use partner sites or apps.

Data Storage

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data beyond what AdMob collects for ad serving. All calculator inputs and results remain on your device and are not sent to any servers.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob – for displaying advertisements. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.
