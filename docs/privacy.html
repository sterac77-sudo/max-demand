Privacy Policy – Drag Racing Toolbox

Last updated: 9 November 2025

Drag Racing Toolbox ("the App") is provided by Intolerance Racing. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

1. Summary

The App shows ads via Google AdMob. Calculator inputs and results are processed only on your device and never sent to our servers.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display banner and interstitial ads. AdMob may collect data such as:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address & general location (city-level)
  • App usage (e.g., screens viewed, ad interactions)
  • Device type, language, OS version

Google uses this data to provide, maintain, and improve ad services, including frequency capping, aggregated reporting, and detecting fraud. For details see Google Privacy Policy and AdMob Data Usage.

3. Personal Data

We do not collect names, email addresses, precise locations, contacts, media, or other personally identifiable information.

4. Data Storage & Transfer

Ad data flows directly between your device and Google AdMob. We do not operate back-end servers for this App. Google may process data on servers in multiple regions.

5. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will cooperate within AdMob's policies.

6. Security

Because we do not store user data, traditional data breach risks are minimized. Ad traffic security is handled by Google.

7. Policy Changes

Updates may occur for compliance or feature changes. The "Last updated" date will reflect revisions.

8. Contact

Questions or concerns: open an issue at GitHub Repository.
