Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 November 2025

Max Demand Calculator ("the App") is provided by Seaspray Electrical. This policy describes how the web version and Android app handle data.

Android app

The Android app does not collect, store, or share personal data. It does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third‑party trackers. All calculations run locally on your device.

Web version

The web version hosted at sterac77-sudo.github.io/max-demand/ uses privacy‑friendly analytics to understand aggregate usage:

  • Plausible Analytics – a lightweight, cookie‑less analytics service that does not collect personal data. See Plausible’s privacy policy.
  • CountAPI – a simple pageview counter storing only counts, not personal data.

No cookies or cross‑site tracking identifiers are used by the App.

Contact

For any privacy questions, please open an issue on the project’s GitHub repository: github.com/sterac77-sudo/max-demand.
